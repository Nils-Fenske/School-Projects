In its New Home at Seattle Center, KEXP will be an arts organization like no other, a hub where music lovers, artists, and the greater KEXP Community can share the joy of “music that matters” in deeper, more meaningful ways. Fueled by its commitment to new sounds and one-of-a-kind experiences, KEXP will expand its offerings and services-on-air, online, and in its New Home-to connect more people to more art and music, across Seattle and around the world.
