After five years in the making, tomorrow marks the public Grand Opening of KEXP’s New Home at Seattle Center. To mark the occasion, KEXP is hosting a free, all-ages celebration from Noon until 9:00 p.m. that will include performances, broadcast live on 90.3 FM Seattle and around the world on KEXP.org, from a dozen artists, including Sharon Jones & the Dap-Kings, Bob Mould, Strand of Oaks, Deep Sea Diver, and more.
