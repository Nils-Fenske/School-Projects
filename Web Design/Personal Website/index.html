About

My target audience is anyone who likes corvettes, hotwheels, or collecting items in general. I hope to inspire them to collect or show off an existing collection of theres as I have done. I am hoping kids of about 5 years and older and adults of all ages will find my collection interesting.

Mission

The mission of this website is to show off my collection of miniature corvette hotwheels. I have been collecting them since I was very young when I received my first one from my father. I continue to search the toy section at almost any store I visit in search of another model I don't yet have. On this website people will get to view my corvettes by year, color, or all at once. Visistors may also read about my collection and leave comments.
